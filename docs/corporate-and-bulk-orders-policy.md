# Corporate and Bulk Orders Policy

This document covers Landmark Group's process for corporate and bulk purchases, distinct from standard retail orders, across Lifestyle, Max, Splash, Home Centre, Centrepoint, and Babyshop in the UAE, KSA, and India. It applies to businesses, event organizers, and institutions purchasing above standard retail quantities.

## Order Thresholds for Bulk Pricing

Bulk pricing tiers begin at a minimum order of 25 units of the same SKU (or size/color run within the same product line) or a total order value of AED 5,000 / SAR 5,000 / INR 100,000, whichever threshold the order crosses first. Orders meeting either threshold qualify for tiered bulk discounts of 8% (25-99 units or AED 5,000-14,999), 12% (100-249 units or AED 15,000-49,999), and 18% (250+ units or AED 50,000+), calculated off the full retail price before any other promotional discount is applied. Bulk pricing cannot be combined with Landmark Rewards points redemption or with active seasonal sale discounts, since bulk pricing is itself already treated as the applicable discount mechanism for that order.

Requests below the 25-unit / AED 5,000 threshold are directed to standard retail checkout and are not eligible for bulk pricing, though customers placing frequent smaller orders can request an account review to determine if rolling volume qualifies them for a corporate account (see below).

## Dedicated Account Manager Assignment

Corporate customers whose confirmed trailing 12-month spend exceeds AED 50,000 (or SAR/INR equivalent) are assigned a dedicated account manager from Landmark's B2B sales team. The account manager serves as the single point of contact for quote requests, stock availability checks across brands, and escalation of any delivery or quality issue, replacing the standard CX ticketing queue for that account. Accounts below this threshold are serviced through a shared corporate sales inbox with a standard 2-business-day response commitment, rather than a named manager.

Account manager assignment is reviewed annually; an account whose spend drops below AED 50,000 in a subsequent 12-month period is transitioned back to the shared inbox, with 30 days' notice given to the account's designated purchasing contact ahead of the change.

## Invoicing and Payment Terms

Approved corporate accounts (those that have completed Landmark's corporate account application, including trade license or equivalent business registration verification) are eligible for net-30 invoicing: goods are delivered or made available for collection first, with payment due within 30 calendar days of the invoice date. Net-30 terms require the account to pass a credit check administered by Landmark's finance team and are subject to a credit limit set per account, beyond which further orders require either partial upfront payment or expedited settlement of outstanding invoices.

One-off bulk orders placed by customers without an approved corporate account (for example, a school placing a single large uniform order) require full payment upfront via card, bank transfer, or approved company cheque before the order is confirmed and processed. Upfront bulk orders do not have access to net-30 terms regardless of order size, since credit terms are extended only to verified, ongoing corporate accounts.

Corporate invoices are issued with VAT (UAE/KSA) or GST (India) itemized in accordance with local tax regulations, and accounts requiring purchase-order-matched invoicing can request this format from their account manager at the time of order placement.

## Corporate Gifting and Customization

Landmark's corporate gifting service, available through Lifestyle, Home Centre, and Babyshop, supports branded packaging (custom-printed boxes, ribbon, and inserts bearing the client's logo) and product customization (embroidery or engraving on select apparel, drinkware, and gift-set items) for orders of 50 units or more. Customization requires a minimum lead time of 10 business days from artwork approval to fulfillment, and a digital proof is shared with the corporate contact for sign-off before production begins.

Branded packaging and customization are quoted separately from the underlying bulk product discount and are not eligible for the standard 8-18% bulk pricing tiers, since customization is treated as a value-added service line rather than a unit-price discount.

## Returns and Cancellations on Bulk Orders

Bulk and corporate orders follow a modified returns process: unopened, unbranded stock can be returned within 14 days of delivery for a full refund less a 5% restocking fee, reduced from the standard consumer 30-day window in recognition of the volume involved. Customized or branded items are non-returnable once production has begun, consistent with the standard exclusion on made-to-order goods across all Landmark brands.
