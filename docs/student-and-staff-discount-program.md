# Student and Staff Discount Program

Landmark Group offers a standing discount program for verified students and staff of partner institutions, available across Lifestyle, Max, Splash, Home Centre, Centrepoint, and Babyshop in the UAE, KSA, and India. This document covers eligibility verification, discount value, category exclusions, usage frequency, and how the discount interacts with Landmark Rewards.

## Eligibility and Verification

The program is open to two groups: enrolled students aged 16 and above at accredited schools, colleges, and universities, and staff members of Landmark's registered corporate and government partner organizations (a list maintained by the partnerships team and updated quarterly). Eligibility is not self-declared; it is confirmed through a third-party verification service (currently UNiDAYS for students and a comparable staff-ID verification vendor for corporate partners) that Landmark integrates into the checkout flow and the in-store verification kiosk.

To verify, a customer creates a one-time profile with the verification vendor using their institutional email address or uploads a photo of a current, unexpired student or staff ID card. Verification is typically instant when an institutional email is used, and can take up to 24 hours when manual document review is required. Once verified, the customer receives a unique discount code valid for 12 months, after which re-verification is required to confirm continued eligibility (for example, a student who has since graduated is no longer eligible once the 12-month window lapses).

## Discount Value

Verified students and staff receive a flat 10% discount off full-priced merchandise, applied either by entering the discount code at online checkout or by presenting the verification confirmation (digital pass or ID) at the till in-store. The discount is not tiered by brand; it applies uniformly across all six participating brands at the same 10% rate.

## Category Exclusions

The student and staff discount does not apply to:

1. Items already marked down or on sale, including Max clearance stock and end-of-season sale items across any brand, since discounts do not stack on top of an existing markdown.
2. Gift cards and e-gift cards, in line with the standard Landmark policy that gift cards are excluded from all promotional discounts.
3. Beauty and fragrance products sold through Lifestyle's beauty hall concessions, which are operated under separate brand-partner pricing agreements that do not permit third-party discounting.
4. Delivery, click-and-collect, and other service fees, which are charged at standard rates regardless of discount eligibility.

## Frequency of Use

The discount code can be used once per calendar month per verified account, applied to a single transaction of any size (there is no minimum basket value). It cannot be split across multiple smaller transactions in the same month to claim the discount twice, and the checkout system automatically blocks a second redemption attempt within the same 30-day window from first use. Unused months do not roll over or bank toward a larger future discount.

## Stacking with Landmark Rewards

The student and staff discount stacks with Landmark Rewards membership. A Landmark Rewards member who applies the 10% student/staff discount still earns points on the final, post-discount amount paid, consistent with the standard rule that points accrue on the post-discount, pre-shipping price. Points redemption at checkout can also be combined with the student/staff discount in the same transaction — a customer can apply both the 10% code and a points-based instant discount together, since the two are treated as independent, non-competing discount mechanisms by the checkout engine.

Tier-based benefits (such as a Gold member's free shipping or a Platinum member's priority CX line) remain fully available alongside the student/staff discount, since those are service benefits rather than price discounts and are never affected by which discount codes are applied to the merchandise total.

## Re-Verification and Fraud Prevention

Landmark's verification vendor periodically re-screens active profiles against institutional enrollment or employment records to catch lapsed eligibility before the 12-month renewal date, particularly for staff accounts where an employee may have left the partner organization. Accounts found to be using an expired or fraudulent verification are deactivated from the program, any discount applied to an in-progress order is reversed at the point of sale, and repeat abuse is escalated to the CX fraud review team, which may bar the account from future program enrollment.
