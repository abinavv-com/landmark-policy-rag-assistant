# Price Match Guarantee Policy

This document covers Landmark Group's price match guarantee against named external retail competitors. It is distinct from the internal cross-store price alignment described in the Store and Online Service FAQ, which covers matching prices between Landmark's own brand storefronts (for example, an online Lifestyle price versus a physical Lifestyle store price) rather than matching an outside competitor.

## Qualifying Competitors

The price match guarantee applies only to the identical product (same SKU, size, and color) listed at a lower price on the following named competitor storefronts, depending on region:

- **UAE and KSA**: Namshi (first-party listings only), and Noon.com where the item is sold and shipped directly by Noon rather than a third-party marketplace seller.
- **India**: Reliance Trends and Pantaloons, for comparable apparel and footwear categories where both retailers carry the identical branded product.

The competitor's price must be a standard, currently live listed price at the time of the claim, not a screenshot from an expired promotion or a price the customer recalls from memory.

## What Is Excluded

The following are explicitly excluded from price matching, regardless of brand or region:

1. Third-party marketplace sellers on any platform, including marketplace listings on Noon, Amazon.ae, or Amazon.in, since Landmark cannot verify the authenticity, condition, or stock status of marketplace-fulfilled inventory.
2. Flash sales, deal-of-the-day pricing, and time-boxed clearance events run by a competitor, since these are temporary promotional prices rather than standard listed prices.
3. Prices at any other Landmark Group brand (Lifestyle, Max, Splash, Home Centre, Centrepoint, Babyshop). Cross-brand or cross-store price differences within Landmark Group are handled exclusively under the separate internal price-match process described in the Store and Online Service FAQ, not under this competitor guarantee.
4. Bundled offers, buy-one-get-one promotions, or any pricing that includes a free gift or added item not present in the Landmark listing, since these are not a direct price-for-price comparison.
5. Competitor prices outside the UAE, KSA, and India markets, since Landmark's price match guarantee only operates within the markets it directly serves.

## Claim Window

A price match claim must be submitted within 7 days of the Landmark purchase date, aligned with Landmark's standard internal price-match window. Claims submitted after 7 days are not eligible, even if the lower competitor price was still live at the time of the late claim.

## Required Proof

Customers must submit one of the following as proof at the time of the claim:

- A dated screenshot of the competitor's live product page showing the price, product name, and a visible date/timestamp.
- A direct link to the competitor's live listing, which the CX team will independently verify at the time the claim is reviewed.

Claims without verifiable proof, or where the CX team cannot independently confirm the competitor's price is still live and matches the identical SKU, size, and color, are declined.

## How the Refund Is Issued

Approved price match claims are refunded as the price difference only (not the full order value), issued to the original payment method within 5-7 business days, consistent with Landmark's standard refund timelines by payment method. Customers who prefer an immediate resolution instead of waiting for a card refund may opt to receive the difference as instant store credit, which carries no bonus value under this competitor guarantee (unlike the 10% bonus applied to standard return-refund store credit).

Price match claims do not affect Landmark Rewards points already earned on the original purchase; points are calculated on the amount actually paid at the time of purchase and are not retroactively adjusted downward when a price match refund is issued.
