# Employee Purchase and Referral Program

This document covers the Landmark Group employee discount program and the friends-and-family referral program, both of which apply to permanent employees of Landmark Group entities across the UAE, KSA, and India. It is distinct from the Student and Staff Discount Program, which is a customer-facing 10% discount for verified students and for staff of Landmark's external corporate and government partner organizations; this document instead covers the internal discount extended to Landmark's own workforce, and the referral incentive employees can earn by bringing in new customers.

## Employee Discount Rate and Eligibility

Confirmed permanent employees of Landmark Group receive a **20% discount** off full-priced merchandise across all six brands (Lifestyle, Max, Splash, Home Centre, Centrepoint, Babyshop), applied via a unique employee discount code linked to their HR employee ID, usable both online and in-store. This is double the 10% rate extended to external partner staff and students under the Student and Staff Discount Program, reflecting the difference between a customer-facing partnership benefit and an internal workforce benefit.

The discount activates only after an employee completes their **90-day probationary period**, consistent with Landmark's standard HR probation policy across all markets. Employees on probation do not have access to the discount code, and attempting to apply an employee discount code before the 90-day activation date results in an automatic checkout rejection. Once activated, the discount remains available for the duration of the employee's active employment and is deactivated automatically by an HR system feed on the employee's last working day, so no manual revocation step is required.

## Category Exclusions

The employee discount does not apply to items already on sale or clearance, gift cards, beauty-hall concession products operated under separate brand-partner pricing agreements, or made-to-order and customized items, mirroring the same exclusion list used across the Student and Staff Discount Program and other promotional discounts. It also does not apply to delivery, click-and-collect, alteration, or other service fees.

## Purchase Caps

To prevent the employee discount from being used for informal resale or bulk arbitrage, each employee's discounted purchases are capped at **AED 3,000 (or SAR/INR equivalent) per calendar month** across all brands combined, tracked centrally against the employee's HR ID regardless of which store or storefront the purchase is made through. Purchases beyond this monthly cap are still permitted but are charged at full price without the 20% discount applied. The cap resets on the first day of each calendar month and does not roll over unused allowance from a prior month.

## Friends-and-Family Referral Program

Employees can also participate in the Friends-and-Family Referral Program, under which an employee shares a personal referral code with a friend or family member who does not already hold a Landmark Rewards account. When the referred person creates a new Landmark Rewards account using that code and completes a qualifying first purchase of at least AED 200 within 60 days of account creation, the referring employee receives a referral bonus of **500 Landmark Rewards points** (equivalent to AED 50 of redemption value), credited to the employee's own Landmark Rewards balance within 5 business days of the qualifying purchase being confirmed.

There is no cap on the number of successful referrals an employee can generate in a given month, since the program is designed to reward genuine new-customer acquisition rather than to be capped as a cost-control measure the way the discount itself is.

## Abuse Prevention Rules

Several rules protect both programs from misuse:

1. **No self-referral.** An employee cannot use their own referral code to create a second Landmark Rewards account for themselves, a family member's existing account, or any account sharing the employee's own registered payment method or delivery address. Referral bonuses paid out on a detected self-referral are clawed back from the employee's points balance, and repeat attempts are escalated to HR.
2. **No discount code sharing.** The employee discount code is non-transferable and tied to the individual employee's HR ID; an employee found sharing their code with a non-employee for use at checkout has the discount privilege suspended for a minimum of 90 days on a first offense, and permanently revoked on a second offense.
3. **Purchase cap enforcement is automatic.** The AED 3,000 monthly cap is enforced at the point-of-sale system level and cannot be manually overridden by store staff, including managers, ensuring consistent application across all locations.
4. **Referral qualifying purchase must be genuine retail activity.** Bulk, corporate, or wholesale-scale purchases routed through the referral code to inflate bonus payouts are excluded from qualifying, since the referral program is scoped to ordinary retail transactions rather than the corporate and bulk order channel described in the Corporate and Bulk Orders Policy.

## Interaction With Landmark Rewards and Landmark+

Employee discount purchases still earn standard Landmark Rewards points on the post-discount amount paid, consistent with the group-wide rule that points accrue on the price actually paid rather than the pre-discount ticket price. The employee discount does not stack with the Landmark+ 5% membership discount; where an employee also holds a Landmark+ subscription, the checkout system applies whichever single discount is larger for that order, following the same non-stacking logic used between Landmark+ and the Student and Staff Discount Program.
