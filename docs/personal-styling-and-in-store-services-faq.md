# Personal Styling and In-Store Services FAQ

This document covers personal styling appointments, alteration and tailoring services, and in-store beauty and grooming services offered across Landmark Group brands (Lifestyle, Max, Splash, Home Centre, Centrepoint, Babyshop) in the UAE, KSA, and India.

## Personal Styling Appointments

Personal styling is currently offered at Lifestyle and Centrepoint flagship and large-format stores, where a dedicated styling consultant helps a customer build a look across formalwear, occasion wear, or seasonal wardrobe refresh categories. Max, Splash, Home Centre, and Babyshop do not run a dedicated personal styling desk, though Home Centre store associates provide informal decor and furniture-pairing advice on the shop floor without requiring a booked appointment.

Booking is done through the "Book a Styling Session" option under the Services tab on the Lifestyle or Centrepoint app, or by asking a store associate to book a slot on the customer's behalf during a store visit. Sessions run approximately 45-60 minutes and are available at flagship locations in major malls across Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Riyadh, Jeddah, and the larger Indian metro stores; smaller-format stores do not currently host a styling consultant on-site.

A standard styling session is complimentary for all customers, regardless of Landmark Rewards tier, since it is treated as a sales-enablement service rather than a paid add-on. Landmark Rewards Platinum members receive an additional benefit under their tier: one complimentary personal shopping session per year at a participating Lifestyle or Centrepoint flagship store, as already described in the Loyalty Program FAQ. This Platinum personal shopping session is a more extensive, one-on-one experience than the standard styling appointment, typically including pre-appointment outfit curation based on the member's past purchase history and a private fitting room reserved for the full session, whereas the standard styling session is shop-floor based and does not include pre-curation.

## Alteration and Tailoring Services

Basic alterations (hemming trousers, adjusting a waistband, taking in or letting out a jacket at the seams) are available at Lifestyle and Centrepoint stores that stock formalwear, through an in-store or partner tailoring counter. Turnaround time is typically 3-5 business days for standard alterations, or a 24-hour rush service where available at flagship stores for an additional fee. Alteration requests must be made in-store at the time of purchase or during a return visit with the item and receipt; alterations cannot currently be booked or requested through the app or website.

Landmark Rewards Platinum members receive complimentary alterations at Lifestyle formalwear counters as a standing tier benefit, covering standard hemming and basic fit adjustments at no charge; Silver and Gold members pay the standard alteration fee, which varies by garment type and complexity and is quoted by the tailoring counter at drop-off. This complimentary Platinum benefit does not extend to Centrepoint's tailoring counter, since Centrepoint's in-store tailoring is run through a third-party partner rather than a Lifestyle-operated counter, and third-party partner pricing is not covered by the Landmark Rewards tier benefit structure.

Home Centre does not offer garment alteration (not being an apparel-focused brand) but does offer a paid made-to-measure curtain and upholstery service at select large-format stores, with a typical 10-15 business day turnaround depending on fabric availability; this is a separate service line quoted and scheduled directly with the Home Centre soft-furnishings desk rather than through the styling or alterations channel used by the apparel brands.

## In-Store Beauty and Grooming Services

Lifestyle's beauty hall, present at flagship and large-format stores, offers complimentary makeup application sessions when a customer purchases a minimum qualifying value of beauty products (typically AED 150 or local currency equivalent) during the same visit, applied by a brand consultant from the counter whose products are being purchased. These sessions are brand-counter specific rather than a general Lifestyle-operated service, meaning availability, technique, and product range depend on which beauty brand counter the customer is engaging with, and scheduling is handled directly at the counter rather than through a centralized booking system.

Standalone paid grooming services (such as a full facial, brow threading, or hairstyling) are not offered directly by Lifestyle beauty halls; where such services exist within a mall location, they are run by an independent tenant and are not part of the Landmark Group service offering or covered by any Landmark Rewards benefit. Customers asking about facials, threading, or salon services at a Lifestyle location should be directed to check the mall directory rather than the Lifestyle store, since CX cannot book or vouch for third-party mall tenant services.

## Cancellations and No-Shows

Styling appointments and Platinum personal shopping sessions can be rescheduled or cancelled free of charge up to 2 hours before the scheduled slot through the app or by calling the specific store directly. Repeated no-shows (three or more within a rolling 6-month period) may result in the store requiring a confirmed booking through CX rather than self-service app booking for that customer's future appointments, as a measure to keep styling consultant capacity available for customers who reliably attend.

## Service Availability Disclaimer

Personal styling, alterations, and beauty hall services are subject to the specific store's format and footfall tier; not every Lifestyle or Centrepoint location carries a styling consultant, tailoring counter, or full beauty hall. Customers should check service availability for their nearest store via the store locator's "Services" filter on the app before planning a visit around one of these services, since smaller neighborhood-format stores typically carry a reduced service set compared to mall flagship locations.
