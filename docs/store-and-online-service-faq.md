# Store and Online Service FAQ

This document covers click-and-collect, price-match, and gift card policies across Landmark Group brands (Lifestyle, Max, Splash, Home Centre, Centrepoint, Babyshop).

## Click-and-Collect

Click-and-collect allows customers to place an order online and pick it up at a participating store instead of receiving home delivery. Orders placed before 2:00 PM local time are typically ready for pickup the same day; orders placed after 2:00 PM are ready by 12:00 PM the following day. The customer receives an SMS and email notification once the order is confirmed ready at the selected store.

Click-and-collect is available at all Lifestyle, Max, Splash, and Centrepoint stores, and at select larger-format Home Centre stores that maintain sufficient on-site stockroom capacity; smaller Home Centre express-format stores do not currently support click-and-collect due to limited backroom space. Babyshop supports click-and-collect at all standalone stores but not at in-mall shop-in-shop counters.

Orders held for pickup are reserved for 5 calendar days from the ready-for-pickup notification. If not collected within this window, the order is automatically cancelled and refunded to the original payment method within 3-5 business days. There is no shipping fee for click-and-collect regardless of order value, and it counts toward Landmark Rewards points identically to a home-delivery order.

To collect, the customer must present the order confirmation (digital or printed) along with a valid ID matching the name on the order, or a valid Landmark Rewards membership card/app profile as an alternative identity check.

## Price-Match Policy

Landmark brands offer a price-match guarantee against listed prices at other authorized Landmark Group storefronts (i.e., matching the online price if a physical Lifestyle store is charging more for the identical SKU, color, and size, or vice versa) within 7 days of purchase. Price-match requests must be submitted with proof of the lower price (a screenshot with visible date/time, or an in-store price tag photo) via the CX chat or in-store customer service desk.

Price-matching does not extend to third-party marketplaces, unauthorized resellers, or competitor retailers outside the Landmark Group. It also does not apply against the brand's own future markdowns or seasonal sale prices that go into effect after the original purchase date — a customer who buys at full price and sees the same item marked down two weeks later during a seasonal sale is not eligible for a retroactive price adjustment, since seasonal sales are treated as a distinct pricing event rather than a price correction.

Approved price-match claims are refunded as the price difference to the original payment method within 5-7 business days, or as instant store credit if the customer opts for immediate resolution over waiting for a card refund.

## Gift Cards

Landmark Group gift cards (physical and e-gift card formats) are valid across all six participating brands and do not expire, in line with regional consumer protection regulations in the UAE and India that prohibit expiry dates on prepaid gift instruments. Gift cards can be purchased in-store or online in fixed denominations (AED 50, 100, 250, 500, 1000, and local currency equivalents) or as a custom amount within a AED 50-2,000 range for e-gift cards purchased online.

Gift cards cannot be reloaded once the balance reaches zero; a new card or e-gift card must be purchased for further use. Partial balances remain usable across multiple transactions until fully depleted, and the remaining balance is always visible in the customer's Landmark Rewards app under the "Gift Cards" tab if the card was registered to their account at time of purchase or activation.

Lost physical gift cards that were registered to a Landmark Rewards account can be frozen and the remaining balance transferred to a replacement card, subject to a AED 10 replacement processing fee. Unregistered physical gift cards that are lost cannot be recovered, replaced, or refunded, since Landmark has no way to verify ownership or remaining balance without registration.

Gift cards cannot be purchased using another gift card as the payment method, and cannot be redeemed toward the purchase of additional gift cards, to prevent value-laundering between accounts.
