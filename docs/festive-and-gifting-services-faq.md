# Festive and Gifting Services FAQ

This document covers gift-wrapping, gift receipts, group gifting and registry features, and corporate holiday gifting options across Landmark Group brands (Lifestyle, Max, Splash, Home Centre, Centrepoint, Babyshop) in the UAE, KSA, and India.

## Gift-Wrapping Availability and Cost

Gift-wrapping is available both in-store and as a checkout option for online orders, though availability and cost vary slightly by brand given differences in packaging format:

- **Lifestyle and Centrepoint**: Gift-wrapping is complimentary in-store at the till. Online, gift-wrapping can be added at checkout for a flat AED 10 (or SAR 10 / INR 150) fee per gift-wrapped item.
- **Max and Splash**: Gift-wrapping is available in-store for a flat AED 5 fee (waived during the November-January festive window each year). Online gift-wrap follows the same AED 10 per-item fee as Lifestyle and Centrepoint.
- **Home Centre**: Gift-wrapping is available for smaller décor and homeware items but not for furniture or large appliances, given practical packaging constraints. The fee is AED 15 per item both in-store and online, reflecting the bulkier packaging materials used for homeware.
- **Babyshop**: Gift-wrapping is complimentary both in-store and online year-round, in line with the brand's positioning around baby shower and newborn gifting occasions.

Gift-wrapping requests for online orders must be made at checkout before the order is placed; it cannot be added retroactively to an order already in fulfillment.

## Gift Receipts vs. Standard Receipts

Customers can request a gift receipt in place of, or in addition to, a standard receipt, either at the till or by selecting the "This is a gift" checkbox at online checkout. A gift receipt lists the item name, size/color, and store or order reference but omits the price paid, allowing the recipient to process a return or exchange without seeing the original purchase value. Gift receipts follow the same return eligibility rules as the standard Returns and Exchange Policy (30-day standard window, with the shorter 14-day window on Max sale items and 21-day window on Splash and Home Centre sale items still applying based on the underlying purchase date). A gift receipt is not a separate proof of purchase category with different return terms — it is the same transaction with price information suppressed for the recipient's benefit.

Refunds against a gift receipt are issued as store credit rather than to the original payment method by default, since the person returning the item is typically not the original payer; the original purchaser can instead request a refund to their original payment method directly, using their order confirmation rather than the gift receipt.

## Group Gifting and Registry Features

Lifestyle, Home Centre, and Babyshop support online registries for major life events: Lifestyle and Home Centre jointly support wedding registries (spanning homeware, furniture, and formalwear accessories across both brands under one shared registry link), while Babyshop supports baby registries for expecting parents. A registry is created by the account holder specifying desired items from participating brands' catalogs, and the registry link can be shared with family and friends, who can purchase individual items or contribute toward a larger item's cost through partial group gifting.

Baby registries created on Babyshop can cross-reference items from Lifestyle's baby apparel range and Home Centre's nursery furniture range within the same registry link, since these categories are commonly gifted together for a newborn. Contributions toward a registry item do not earn Landmark Rewards points for the contributor unless the contributor is a Landmark Rewards member checking out under their own account; points always accrue to the purchasing account, not the registry owner's account.

Registries remain active for 12 months from creation, after which unpurchased items are archived and the registry link is deactivated; the registry owner can request an extension via CX before expiry if the event date has moved.

## Corporate Holiday Gifting

Businesses looking to place bulk holiday or festive gift orders for employees or clients can do so through the Landmark Group corporate sales desk, the same channel used for corporate bulk gift card purchases described in the Gift Cards and Vouchers Policy. Corporate gifting orders combining physical products (hampers, homeware sets, or curated apparel bundles) with gift cards are supported as a single consolidated order above a minimum value of AED 5,000, with bulk-order gift-wrapping included at no additional per-item charge for orders meeting this threshold. Corporate gifting orders during the November-January festive peak should be placed at least 15 business days in advance of the required delivery date, since fulfillment slots for bulk hamper assembly are limited during this period and are allocated on a first-confirmed basis.

## Seasonal Gift-Wrap Availability Notice

During the peak festive period (typically mid-November through early January, coinciding with the Landmark Rewards bonus-multiplier campaigns), in-store gift-wrapping counters may temporarily operate on a queue-ticket system at high-footfall stores rather than till-side wrapping, to manage volume. Online gift-wrap requests during this period are unaffected and continue to be fulfilled at the standard per-item fee and timeline.
