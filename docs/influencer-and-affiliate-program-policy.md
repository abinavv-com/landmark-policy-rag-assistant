# Influencer and Affiliate Program Policy

This document covers the Landmark Creator Program, the affiliate and influencer partnership scheme through which content creators earn commission by promoting products from Lifestyle, Max, Splash, Home Centre, Centrepoint, and Babyshop across the UAE, KSA, and India.

## Eligibility and Application

Creators apply through the Landmark Creator Program portal by submitting their primary social handle (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, or Snapchat), audience country breakdown, and at least three examples of prior branded or organic content. Applicants must have a minimum of 5,000 followers on their primary platform and demonstrate an audience with at least 60% concentration in the UAE, KSA, or India to qualify, since the program is scoped to Landmark's operating markets. Applications are reviewed within 10 business days, and approved creators receive a unique tracking link and discount code tied to their account, which is how sales are attributed for commission purposes.

Creators under 18 years of age may apply only with a parent or guardian as the named account holder and payee of record. Landmark reserves the right to decline any application where the audience shows signs of substantial bot or purchased-follower activity, assessed using standard engagement-rate benchmarks (comments-to-followers and likes-to-followers ratios) at the time of review.

## Commission Structure

Commission is calculated on the net sale value (post-discount, pre-shipping, pre-VAT) of orders placed using the creator's tracking link or code within a 14-day attribution window from the click or code entry. Base commission rates vary by product category, reflecting margin differences across the portfolio:

- Fashion apparel (Lifestyle, Max, Splash, Centrepoint): 10% base commission.
- Home and furniture (Home Centre): 6% base commission, reflecting lower category margins on large-ticket furniture items.
- Baby and kids (Babyshop): 8% base commission.
- Beauty and accessories: 12% base commission, the highest standard rate in the program.

Creators are placed into one of three tiers based on trailing 90-day sales volume generated through their link, which increases the base commission rate by a fixed uplift: Rising (under AED 10,000 in trailing 90-day sales, no uplift), Established (AED 10,000-50,000, +2 percentage points across all categories), and Elite (above AED 50,000, +4 percentage points across all categories, plus early access to seasonal lookbooks before public release). Tier status is recalculated on the first business day of each month based on the prior rolling 90-day window, so a creator can move up or down between tiers as their sales performance changes.

## Payout Schedule and Threshold

Commission accrues to a creator's Landmark Creator Program wallet as sales are confirmed (i.e., after the standard 30-day return window on the underlying order has closed, to account for potential returns). Payouts are processed monthly, on the 15th of each month, covering all commission confirmed as of the end of the prior calendar month. The minimum payout threshold is AED 500 (or SAR 500 / INR 10,000 equivalent); balances below this threshold roll over to the following month rather than being paid out. Payments are made via bank transfer to the account details provided at enrollment, and creators are responsible for any tax reporting obligations in their country of residence.

## Content Usage Rights

By participating in the program, creators grant Landmark Group a non-exclusive, royalty-free license to repost, edit, and reuse any content they create featuring Landmark products across Landmark's own social channels, website, and paid advertising for a period of 12 months from the date of original posting. This license does not transfer copyright ownership of the content, and creators retain the right to keep the content on their own channels and to decline renewal of the license after the 12-month period by written notice to the program team. Landmark does not pay a separate licensing fee for this reuse right beyond the standard commission structure, and this is disclosed to creators in the program terms accepted at enrollment.

## Program Termination and Fraud Policy

Landmark reserves the right to suspend or terminate a creator's participation, forfeiting any unpaid commission balance accrued through fraudulent activity, in cases including: use of bot traffic or purchased engagement to inflate attributed sales, self-referral (a creator using their own code to purchase products for personal use and claiming commission on those orders), coordinated code-sharing with unauthorized third parties to bypass tier eligibility checks, or posting content that violates Landmark's brand guidelines or applicable advertising disclosure regulations (such as failing to disclose a paid or affiliate relationship as required by UAE and KSA advertising standards authorities). Creators found in violation are notified in writing and given 5 business days to respond before a final termination decision is made, except in cases of confirmed bot-driven fraud, which result in immediate suspension pending investigation.

## Reapplication

Creators terminated for policy violations other than confirmed fraud may reapply after a 6-month cooling-off period. Creators terminated for confirmed fraudulent activity are permanently barred from reapplying under the same identity, social handle, or payment details.
