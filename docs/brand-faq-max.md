# Max Brand FAQ

Max is Landmark Group's value-fashion brand, built around high sale frequency, frequent clearance cycles, and family-value pricing across the UAE, KSA, and India. This supplementary FAQ, maintained by the Max CX team, addresses questions that come up specifically because of Max's high-volume, fast-turnover retail model.

## Why Max Runs Sales So Often

Max operates on a significantly faster markdown cadence than Lifestyle or Centrepoint, with clearance events running nearly continuously across some category or store cluster at any given time, layered with larger seasonal clearance windows around Ramadan, Eid, back-to-school, and end-of-season changeovers. This is a deliberate part of Max's value-fashion positioning: high volume, frequent new stock intake, and fast sell-through of markdown inventory to keep shelf space turning over for new arrivals. Customers who want first pick of new stock should expect it to move quickly into markdown within a matter of weeks rather than months, which is faster than the markdown cycle at Lifestyle.

## Why Sale Item Returns Are Shorter at Max

Max applies a 14-day return window on sale and clearance items, compared to the standard 30-day window on full-priced Max merchandise, as set out in the Returns and Exchange Policy. This shorter window exists specifically because of Max's clearance volume: at any time, a large share of transactions at Max are markdown purchases, and a 30-day window on that volume would leave clearance inventory in limbo for long stretches, complicating restocking, re-merchandising, and the ability to fully clear a size run before the next markdown cycle begins. The 14-day window lets Max close out clearance stock definitively while still giving customers a fair, if tighter, opportunity to return a sale item that doesn't work out. CX agents should always flag this distinction proactively when a customer asks about returning a discounted Max purchase, since it remains the single most common return-policy question escalated from Max stores.

## Family-Value Multi-Buy Promotions

Max regularly runs family-value multi-buy promotions such as "buy 2 get 1 free" or tiered percentage-off offers on kidswear, basics, and essentials categories, reflecting its positioning toward value-conscious family shoppers. Multi-buy discounts are applied automatically at checkout once qualifying items are in the cart and do not require a separate promo code unless stated on the specific campaign banner. Items purchased under a multi-buy promotion are still eligible for return individually, but the refund reflects the actual discounted price paid for that specific unit under the promotion, not the item's standalone full price, since Landmark Rewards points and refunds are always calculated on the post-discount amount actually paid.

## Max Own-Brand Product Lines

Max carries several store-brand lines across kidswear, basics, and home essentials that are exclusive to Max and not available at Lifestyle, Splash, or Home Centre. These own-brand lines follow the same 30-day full-price and 14-day sale-item return windows as the rest of Max's assortment; Max does not offer an extended warranty beyond the standard footwear and apparel terms set out in the general Warranty Policy, meaning own-brand footwear carries the same 90-day manufacturing-defect warranty applied across Lifestyle, Max, Splash, and Centrepoint. Max's own-brand quality stance is built around consistent, accessible pricing rather than premium materials, and customers should expect the same defect-coverage terms, not an enhanced warranty, on store-brand purchases.

## A Note on Sizing

As covered in the group-wide Size and Fit Guide, Max's size chart runs approximately half a size to a full size smaller than Lifestyle's chart under the same labeled size, most noticeably in shoulder and bust measurements for women's tops. Customers new to Max who normally shop Lifestyle are advised to size up by one size if between sizes, particularly given how frequently Max's fast sale cadence means a size may sell out before a customer can exchange it.
