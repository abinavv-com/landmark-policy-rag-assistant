# Loyalty Program FAQ (Landmark Rewards)

Landmark Rewards is the unified loyalty program covering purchases at Lifestyle, Max, Splash, Home Centre, Centrepoint, and Babyshop under a single membership account.

## How Points Are Earned

Members earn 1 point for every AED 10 (or local currency equivalent: SAR 10, INR 100) spent on eligible purchases, both online and in-store, across all six participating brands. Points are calculated on the post-discount, pre-shipping-fee amount, meaning promotional or clearance purchases still earn points, but only on the discounted price actually paid, and shipping charges themselves do not earn points.

Bonus point multipliers apply during official Landmark Rewards campaign periods (typically 3-4 times per year, such as the Ramadan and Back-to-School campaigns), during which members can earn up to 3x points on qualifying categories. Bonus multipliers are always communicated in advance via app notification and are never retroactively applied to past purchases.

## Cross-Brand Point Pooling

Points earned at any one of the six brands pool into a single unified Landmark Rewards balance and can be redeemed at any of the other five brands. For example, points earned from a Max purchase can be redeemed toward a Home Centre furniture purchase, and points earned at Lifestyle can be redeemed at Babyshop. This cross-brand pooling is a deliberate design choice of the unified rewards program launched to replace the previous brand-siloed schemes, and it is one of the most frequently highlighted benefits in loyalty program marketing. There is no restriction, cap, or conversion penalty when redeeming points earned under one brand at a different brand within the group.

## Redemption

100 points redeem for AED 10 / SAR 10 / INR 100 of purchase value, applied as an instant discount at checkout (online) or at the till (in-store). Members must have a minimum balance of 200 points to redeem, and redemption must be done in increments of 100 points. Points cannot be redeemed for cash and cannot be used to pay for gift cards.

Points can be combined with other running promotions and coupon codes, except during a small number of clearly marked "no additional discount stacking" clearance events, which are flagged on the campaign banner.

## Tier Benefits

Landmark Rewards has three tiers, determined by rolling 12-month spend across all brands combined:

- **Silver** (entry tier, no minimum spend): Standard point earning rate, birthday month discount voucher, access to member-only sale previews 24 hours before public launch.
- **Gold** (AED 3,000+ rolling 12-month spend): All Silver benefits, plus free standard shipping on all online orders regardless of order value, 1.25x point earning rate, and early access to new arrivals 48 hours before public launch.
- **Platinum** (AED 10,000+ rolling 12-month spend): All Gold benefits, plus 1.5x point earning rate, a dedicated CX priority line, complimentary alterations at Lifestyle formalwear counters, and an annual personal shopping session at a participating Lifestyle or Centrepoint flagship store.

Tier status is reviewed and recalculated on a rolling monthly basis based on trailing 12-month spend, meaning a member can move up or down a tier as their spend pattern changes, rather than being locked in for a fixed annual cycle.

## Points Expiry

Points expire 18 months from the date they were earned, on a first-in-first-out basis, meaning the oldest points in a member's balance expire first as new points are earned. Members receive an app and email notification 30 days before any points are due to expire, giving them a window to redeem before losing that batch. Points do not expire while a member holds Gold or Platinum tier status, but revert to the standard 18-month expiry rule immediately if the member's tier drops back to Silver.

## Account and Enrollment

Enrollment is free and can be completed at any till in-store or via the Landmark Rewards tab on any brand's app or website. A single mobile number or email can only be linked to one Landmark Rewards account, and merging two existing accounts (for example, if a customer accidentally created separate accounts under a different phone number) requires a CX request with proof of purchase history for both accounts, processed within 5 business days.
