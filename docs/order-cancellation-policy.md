# Order Cancellation Policy

This document covers cancellation windows, refund handling, and cancellation rules for Click & Collect versus home delivery orders across Landmark Group brands (Lifestyle, Max, Splash, Home Centre, Centrepoint, Babyshop) in the UAE, KSA, and India.

## Cancellation Window Before Dispatch

Orders can be cancelled free of charge, in full or in part, at any point before the order is dispatched from the warehouse. In practice, this breaks down into two phases:

- **Instant cancellation (within 1 hour of placing the order)**: The customer can cancel directly from the "My Orders" section of the app or website with a single tap, and the order is cancelled immediately, before it enters the warehouse picking queue.
- **Standard cancellation (after 1 hour, up to dispatch)**: The customer submits a cancellation request through "My Orders" or via CX chat. Because the order may already be in the picking or packing stage, this request is processed on a best-effort basis; if the warehouse has not yet handed the package to the courier, the cancellation is confirmed within a few hours. Orders already past the packing stage at the time of the request may not be cancellable through this route and instead proceed to dispatch.

## Cancellation Once Shipped

Once an order has been dispatched and handed to the courier, it can no longer be cancelled. At this point, the customer must wait to receive the order and then initiate a standard return through the "My Orders" portal, following the return windows and conditions set out in the Returns and Exchange Policy (30 days full-price, with the shorter 14-day and 21-day sale-item windows applicable at Max and at Splash/Home Centre respectively). Refusing delivery at the door is treated as a return-in-transit and follows the same refund timeline as a standard return once the courier confirms the parcel has been returned to the warehouse.

## How Cancellation Refunds Are Processed

For orders cancelled before dispatch, no refund processing delay applies to the underlying reservation, since no shipment has occurred, but the payment reversal still follows the standard method-based timelines:

- Card payments: 5-7 business days.
- Wallet, UPI, or Landmark Rewards balance payments: 3-5 business days.
- Cash on delivery orders: since no payment has been collected yet at the point of cancellation, there is nothing to refund; the order is simply voided.
- Landmark Rewards points used as partial payment are restored to the account instantly upon cancellation confirmation.

Gift cards or store credit applied to a cancelled order are restored to the customer's account balance instantly, without any processing delay, since these are internal Landmark instruments rather than external payment rails.

## Click & Collect vs. Home Delivery Cancellation

**Click & Collect orders** can be cancelled any time before the order is marked "Ready for Pickup" at the selected store, following the same 1-hour instant / pre-dispatch standard rule above. Once marked ready for pickup, the order cannot be cancelled online; the customer must either collect it and then initiate a standard in-store return, or simply not collect it, in which case it is automatically cancelled and refunded once the 5-calendar-day pickup reservation window (as described in the Store and Online Service FAQ) lapses.

**Home delivery orders** follow the standard pre-dispatch cancellation rule described above, with cancellation no longer possible once the courier has been handed the package.

## Partial Cancellation of Multi-Item Orders

Customers can cancel individual items within a multi-item order without affecting the remaining items, provided the cancellation request for that specific item is submitted before it enters the packing stage. Partially cancelling an order recalculates any order-level threshold benefits (such as the free-shipping minimum or a bundled discount) based on the remaining items only; if the remaining order value falls below the free-shipping threshold after a partial cancellation, the standard shipping fee is charged on the remaining shipment. If a partial cancellation causes a promo code's minimum spend condition to no longer be met, the promo discount is reversed proportionally on the remaining items before the refund for the cancelled item is calculated.

## Cancellations Initiated by Landmark

In rare cases, Landmark may cancel an order or part of an order due to a stock discrepancy discovered after the order was placed but before dispatch. In these cases, the customer is notified immediately by SMS and email, and the full amount paid for the cancelled item(s) is refunded automatically to the original payment method within the standard method-based timelines above, with no action required from the customer.
