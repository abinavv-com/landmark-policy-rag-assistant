# Landmark+ Membership FAQ

Landmark+ is a paid annual membership tier, separate and distinct from the free, points-based Landmark Rewards loyalty program. This document covers Landmark+ benefits, the annual fee, cancellation and refund handling, and how Landmark+ interacts with Landmark Rewards tiers. Landmark+ is available to customers in the UAE, KSA, and India, and applies across Lifestyle, Max, Splash, Home Centre, Centrepoint, and Babyshop.

## What Landmark+ Is (and Isn't)

Landmark Rewards is Landmark's free loyalty program: any customer can enroll at no cost and earn points on every purchase, as described in the loyalty program FAQ. Landmark+ is a separate, paid subscription that a customer opts into on top of their existing (or new) Landmark Rewards account. Enrolling in Landmark+ does not replace or migrate a customer's Landmark Rewards tier, points balance, or points-earning rate — the two programs run in parallel, and a customer can hold any Landmark Rewards tier (Silver, Gold, or Platinum) independently of whether they also subscribe to Landmark+.

## Membership Fee

Landmark+ costs AED 149 / SAR 149 / INR 2,999 per year, billed annually to the payment method on file at signup and renewed automatically each year unless cancelled beforehand. The fee is charged in full at the start of the membership term regardless of how much a member shops during the year; there is no pro-rated monthly option currently offered.

## Landmark+ Benefits

Active Landmark+ members receive:

1. **Free express shipping** on all online orders, with no minimum order value, upgrading standard delivery timelines to the express tier at no extra charge on every order for the duration of the membership.
2. **Early sale access**, with a 48-hour early-access window to EOSS and Ramadan campaign markdowns ahead of the general public, and a separate 24-hour early-access window to routine weekly promotions.
3. **A flat 5% additional discount** on top of the ticketed price at checkout, applied automatically to eligible full-priced and sale merchandise alike (subject to the same exclusions as other discounts: gift cards, beauty-hall concession products, and made-to-order or customized items are excluded from the 5% Landmark+ discount).

## Cancellation and Refund Policy

Landmark+ can be cancelled at any time from the membership settings in the Landmark Rewards app or via a CX request. Cancellation requested within the first 14 days of the initial signup (or of an annual renewal charge) is eligible for a full refund of the AED 149 / SAR 149 / INR 2,999 fee, provided the member has not yet used the express shipping benefit or the 5% discount more than twice during that window. Cancellation requested after the 14-day window, or after benefit usage exceeds that threshold, is not eligible for a refund of the current year's fee; the member retains access to all Landmark+ benefits through the remainder of the already-paid annual term, and the membership simply does not renew at the next billing date.

Members who cancel are not automatically re-enrolled; a lapsed member wishing to rejoin must sign up again at the then-current annual fee.

## How Landmark+ Stacks with Landmark Rewards

Landmark+ benefits are additive to whatever Landmark Rewards tier a member holds. A Landmark Rewards Gold or Platinum member who also subscribes to Landmark+ does not lose their tier-based free shipping or priority CX line; the two programs' benefits are evaluated independently, and the order-level system applies whichever set of benefits is more favorable to the customer for that specific benefit type (for example, a Platinum member already has free shipping from their tier, so the Landmark+ express-shipping upgrade is what adds value, rather than the shipping being free twice over).

The 5% Landmark+ discount stacks with Landmark Rewards points redemption at checkout, since points redemption and the flat percentage discount are calculated as separate, non-competing line items. The 5% Landmark+ discount does not stack with the student and staff discount program; where both would otherwise apply to the same order, the checkout system applies whichever single discount is larger, rather than both simultaneously.

Landmark Rewards points continue to accrue normally for Landmark+ members, calculated on the post-discount amount actually paid after the 5% Landmark+ discount is applied, consistent with the standard points-earning rule used across the loyalty program.
