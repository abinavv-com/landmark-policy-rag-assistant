# Personal Shopper and VIP Concierge Service

This document covers the Landmark VIP Concierge service, a premium, ongoing relationship-manager-style offering for Platinum-tier Landmark Rewards members who also hold an active Landmark+ subscription. It is a distinct service from the standard complimentary personal styling appointment and from the single annual Platinum personal shopping session, both described in the Personal Styling and In-Store Services FAQ — this document exists specifically to draw that distinction clearly, since CX frequently fields confusion between the three service tiers.

## How VIP Concierge Differs From Existing Styling Services

Landmark currently offers three distinct levels of personal shopping support, and it is important CX and customers do not conflate them:

1. **Standard styling appointment** (any customer, any tier): a complimentary, shop-floor-based 45-60 minute session at a Lifestyle or Centrepoint flagship store, bookable by anyone regardless of Landmark Rewards tier.
2. **Platinum annual personal shopping session** (Platinum tier benefit, included at no extra cost): one complimentary, more curated session per year at a participating Lifestyle or Centrepoint flagship, including pre-appointment outfit curation and a private fitting room, as described in the Loyalty Program FAQ and the Personal Styling and In-Store Services FAQ.
3. **VIP Concierge** (this document): an ongoing, year-round relationship with a named dedicated concierge, available only to members who combine Platinum tier with an active Landmark+ subscription, and covering in-home consultations rather than being limited to a single annual in-store appointment.

VIP Concierge is additive to, not a replacement for, the Platinum annual personal shopping session; a qualifying member retains their one complimentary annual session as before and separately has ongoing access to their dedicated concierge throughout the year.

## Eligibility

VIP Concierge is available only to members who meet both conditions simultaneously: Landmark Rewards Platinum tier status (AED 10,000+ rolling 12-month spend) and an active, currently-paid Landmark+ subscription. A member who holds Platinum tier but lets their Landmark+ subscription lapse loses VIP Concierge access at the point of lapse, reverting to the standard Platinum benefits set (including the annual personal shopping session, which is unaffected by Landmark+ status). Conversely, a Landmark+ subscriber who has not yet reached Platinum tier does not qualify for VIP Concierge on the Landmark+ subscription alone; both conditions must be met together.

## What the Concierge Service Covers

A qualifying member is assigned a named dedicated concierge who serves as a single point of contact for shopping needs across all six brands, rather than the member needing to visit separate departments or brands independently. Concierge services include:

- **In-home consultations**: the concierge can visit the member's home, by appointment, to advise on wardrobe curation, seasonal refreshes, or home and furniture selection drawing on Home Centre's catalog, bringing sample items or a curated lookbook rather than requiring a store visit for every touchpoint. In-home visits are currently available in Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Riyadh, Jeddah, and Mumbai, with other metro areas served via video-call consultation instead until in-home coverage expands.
- **Cross-brand sourcing**: the concierge can source and reserve items across Lifestyle, Max, Splash, Home Centre, Centrepoint, and Babyshop on the member's behalf, including checking stock across multiple store locations, which is not something the standard styling or annual-session services do.
- **Priority access to new arrivals**: concierge members receive advance notice of new collection drops ahead of the standard Platinum 48-hour early-access window, typically 72 hours ahead of general availability for categories the member has expressed interest in.
- **Occasion and gifting coordination**: the concierge can assist with sourcing items for a specific event (a wedding, a corporate gifting need, or a milestone birthday), coordinating with the registry and gifting services described in the Festive and Gifting Services FAQ where relevant.

## Booking and Contact

Unlike the self-service booking flow for standard styling appointments, VIP Concierge members contact their assigned concierge directly via a dedicated phone line and messaging channel provided at enrollment, rather than booking through the general app Services tab. Response time for a concierge request is same-day during standard business hours across all three markets. There is no additional fee for concierge services themselves beyond the existing Landmark+ subscription cost; however, items purchased or sourced through the concierge are billed at standard pricing (inclusive of the Landmark+ 5% discount and any applicable Platinum tier benefits) rather than at a separate concierge markup.

## Discontinuation of Service

If a member's Platinum tier status drops (following the standard rolling 12-month spend recalculation described in the Loyalty Program FAQ) or their Landmark+ subscription lapses or is cancelled, VIP Concierge access ends at that point, and the member is notified by their concierge directly rather than only through an automated system message, as a courtesy given the relationship-based nature of the service. A member who regains Platinum status and an active Landmark+ subscription can request concierge reactivation, which is typically reassigned to the same concierge where available, to preserve continuity.
