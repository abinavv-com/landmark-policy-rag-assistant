# Returns and Exchange Policy

This policy applies to all purchases made through Landmark Group online stores and physical retail locations across the UAE, KSA, and India, covering Lifestyle, Max, Splash, Home Centre, Centrepoint, and Babyshop.

## Standard Return Window

Full-priced items purchased at regular price may be returned or exchanged within 30 days of the delivery date (online) or purchase date (in-store), provided the item is unused, unwashed, and in its original condition with all tags attached. Lifestyle and Centrepoint honor the full 30-day window uniformly across all product categories, including footwear and accessories.

Max applies a shorter window for markdown and clearance items: sale items purchased at Max carry only a 14-day return window, compared to the standard 30-day window on full-priced Max merchandise. This is a deliberate policy difference driven by Max's higher clearance sell-through volumes, and customer service agents should flag this distinction proactively when a customer asks about returning a discounted item bought at Max, since it is the single most common policy confusion escalated to the CX team.

Splash and Home Centre both apply a 21-day window on sale items, positioned between Max's 14 days and Lifestyle's full 30 days.

## Condition Requirements

Returned items must meet the following conditions to be eligible for a refund or exchange:

- Original tags, hygiene seals, and brand labels must be intact.
- Footwear must be tried on indoors only, with no visible sole wear.
- Packaging (boxes, dust bags, garment covers) must be included where originally provided.
- Items must be free of odor, stains, makeup transfer, or alteration.

Items failing any of the above checks will be rejected at the returns counter or, for online returns, the refund will be declined and the item shipped back to the customer at their cost.

## Refund Methods

Refunds are processed via the original payment method within 5-7 business days for card payments, and 3-5 business days for wallet or Landmark Rewards balance payments. Cash-on-delivery orders are refunded via bank transfer, which requires the customer to submit IBAN details and typically takes 7-10 business days to settle. Store credit refunds, when selected by the customer instead of original-method refund, are issued instantly and carry a 10% bonus value as an incentive, valid for 12 months.

## Non-Returnable / Exception Categories

The following categories are final sale and cannot be returned or exchanged under any circumstance, regardless of brand:

1. Innerwear, lingerie, and swimwear (hygiene category) once the hygiene seal is broken or removed.
2. Opened or used cosmetics, fragrances, and skincare products from Lifestyle's beauty hall.
3. Customized, monogrammed, or made-to-order items, including tailored garments and engraved jewelry from Lifestyle.
4. Gift cards and e-gift vouchers.
5. Perishable items sold at Home Centre's seasonal decor pop-ups (e.g., fresh floral arrangements).

## Exchange Process

Exchanges for a different size or color are treated as a return-and-rebuy in the same transaction where possible, avoiding a separate refund cycle. In-store exchanges are processed immediately, subject to stock availability at that location. Online exchanges require the customer to initiate a return through the "My Orders" portal and place a new order for the desired variant; Landmark Rewards members receive free exchange shipping labels, while non-members are charged a flat AED 15 (or local currency equivalent) return shipping fee, waived for orders above AED 200.

## International and Cross-Border Purchases

Items purchased through the UAE storefront cannot be returned to an India store and vice versa, due to separate inventory and tax jurisdictions. Customers must initiate returns through the shipping-origin country's designated returns process, including reverse pickup where available in that market.
