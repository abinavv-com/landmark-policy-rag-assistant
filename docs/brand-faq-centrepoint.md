# Centrepoint Brand FAQ

Centrepoint is Landmark Group's multi-brand family fashion concept, bringing together Splash, Shoemart, Lifestyle-adjacent fashion ranges, Babyshop, and Home Centre corners under "5 brands under one roof" in a single store format across the UAE, KSA, and India. This supplementary FAQ, maintained by the Centrepoint CX team, covers questions specific to shopping across sub-brands within one Centrepoint transaction.

## How Returns Work Across Sub-Brands in One Transaction

A single Centrepoint purchase can include items from several of the five participating sub-brand corners (Splash, Shoemart, Lifestyle-adjacent ranges, Babyshop, and Home Centre) rung up under one receipt. Each item can be returned individually, and the return is processed against the sub-brand it was purchased from rather than requiring the entire multi-brand basket to be returned together. In practice this means a customer who bought a Splash top, Shoemart shoes, and a Babyshop item in one Centrepoint transaction can return just the shoes while keeping the rest, with the refund calculated per item at the price paid. As explained in the group-wide Size and Fit Guide, Centrepoint does not have a single unified size chart across its sub-brands, since sub-brand ranges follow their own respective size charts; customers should check the size tab on each product page rather than assuming a Centrepoint-wide sizing standard, particularly when returning or exchanging for a different size.

## Family-Bundle Promotions

Centrepoint regularly runs family-bundle promotions that span multiple sub-brand corners in one transaction, such as discounts for purchasing a Babyshop item alongside a Splash or Shoemart item in the same basket, reflecting Centrepoint's core positioning as a one-stop destination for family shopping trips. These bundle discounts apply automatically at checkout once the qualifying combination of sub-brand items is in the cart. If one item from a bundled transaction is returned, the remaining items' bundle-discounted price is recalculated to reflect the standalone or next-applicable-tier discount, and any refund on the returned item is based on the price actually attributed to it under the original bundle terms.

## Why Centrepoint Honors the Full 30-Day Window on Sale Items

Unlike Max, Splash standalone, and Home Centre, which apply shorter 14-day or 21-day windows on sale items, Centrepoint honors the full 30-day return window uniformly across all product categories, including sale and clearance items, as set out in the Returns and Exchange Policy. This is a deliberate positioning choice: Centrepoint markets itself around family trust and convenience, serving parents and families making multiple purchases across several sub-brand corners in a single visit, often for children who may need a different size within weeks. A shorter sale-return window would work against that family-first trust positioning, particularly for Babyshop and kids' categories carried within Centrepoint stores, so Centrepoint extends the same full window Lifestyle offers rather than the tighter windows used by the standalone value-fashion brands.

## Loyalty Across the Five Brands

Purchases at Centrepoint earn Landmark Rewards points at the standard rate regardless of which sub-brand corner the item came from, and those points pool into the same unified balance shared across all six Landmark Group brands. A family shopping across Splash, Babyshop, and Home Centre corners within one Centrepoint visit earns and can redeem points as a single combined balance, with no need to track points separately by sub-brand.
