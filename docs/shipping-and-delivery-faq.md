# Shipping and Delivery FAQ

## Delivery Timelines by Region

**UAE**: Standard delivery within Dubai, Abu Dhabi, and Sharjah takes 1-2 business days. Delivery to the Northern Emirates (Ras Al Khaimah, Fujairah, Ajman, Umm Al Quwain) takes 2-3 business days. Express same-day delivery is available in Dubai and Abu Dhabi for orders placed before 12:00 PM local time, subject to a AED 25 express fee.

**Saudi Arabia**: Standard delivery to Riyadh, Jeddah, and Dammam takes 2-4 business days. Delivery to smaller towns and remote regions can take 5-7 business days due to reliance on third-party regional carriers outside the three major metro hubs.

**India**: Metro cities (Mumbai, Delhi NCR, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Chennai, Kolkata, Pune) receive standard delivery within 3-5 business days. Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities take 5-8 business days. Certain remote pin codes, particularly in the Northeast states and parts of Jammu & Kashmir, are serviced on a 8-12 business day timeline and are flagged with an "extended delivery area" notice at checkout before the order is placed.

**Other Gulf markets (Kuwait, Bahrain, Oman, Qatar)**: Standard delivery takes 4-6 business days, since these markets are served from the UAE regional distribution center rather than a local warehouse.

## Shipping Costs

Standard shipping is free for orders above AED 200 (or local currency equivalent: SAR 200, INR 1,499, depending on market). Orders below this threshold incur a flat shipping fee of AED 15 / SAR 15 / INR 99. Landmark Rewards Gold and Platinum tier members receive free standard shipping regardless of order value, as one of the core loyalty benefits.

Express and same-day delivery options, where available, always carry an additional fee on top of standard shipping charges and are not waived by loyalty tier status.

Click-and-collect (order online, pick up in store) is always free regardless of order value or membership tier, since it does not use the last-mile courier network.

## Order Tracking

Once an order is dispatched from the warehouse, customers receive an SMS and email with a tracking link and the assigned courier partner's name. Tracking status updates through four stages: Order Confirmed, Dispatched, Out for Delivery, and Delivered. Real-time GPS-based "out for delivery" tracking with a live map is available only in UAE metro areas for now; other markets show stage-based status updates without live location tracking.

Tracking information typically appears within 2-4 hours of dispatch confirmation, and delays in tracking updates do not necessarily indicate a delivery delay, since courier partner scanning cadence varies.

## Delayed or Lost Package Process

If an order has not arrived within the maximum stated delivery window for its region (e.g., 3 business days past the upper bound for UAE metro, 5 business days past the upper bound for India Tier-2/3), the customer should first check the tracking link for the latest scan event, then contact CX via chat or the helpline with the order number.

CX will open a carrier investigation ticket, which typically resolves within 3-5 business days. If the investigation confirms the package is lost in transit, Landmark issues a full refund to the original payment method or, if the customer prefers, ships a replacement order at no additional cost, subject to stock availability. Customers are never asked to pay for a replacement shipment for a carrier-confirmed lost package.

If a package shows as "Delivered" but the customer states it was not received, CX opens a proof-of-delivery request with the courier (signature or photo evidence where available) before proceeding with a refund or replacement decision, typically within 5-7 business days, to rule out miss-delivery to a neighboring address or porch theft.

## Address and Delivery Attempt Rules

Couriers attempt delivery up to three times before returning a package to the origin warehouse as undeliverable. Customers receive an SMS notification before each attempt. Orders returned as undeliverable after three failed attempts are automatically refunded to the original payment method within 7 business days of the warehouse receiving the returned package, minus the original shipping fee if the order was below the free-shipping threshold.
