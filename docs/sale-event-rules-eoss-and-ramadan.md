# Sale Event Rules: EOSS and Ramadan Campaigns

This document sets out the specific rules governing Landmark Group's two largest recurring sale events, the End of Season Sale (EOSS) and the Ramadan campaign, across Lifestyle, Max, Splash, Home Centre, Centrepoint, and Babyshop in the UAE, KSA, and India. These events run under modified terms compared to standard day-to-day promotions; where a rule below is not addressed, the standard policy in the returns, loyalty, and store-service documents applies unchanged.

## Event Calendar and Scope

EOSS runs twice yearly, typically for a 10-14 day window in early January and again in July, marking the transition between seasonal collections. The Ramadan campaign runs for the duration of the Islamic holy month plus a one-week Eid extension, with exact dates shifting annually per the lunar calendar. Both events apply storewide across all six brands, though individual brands set their own category-level markdown depths within the event.

## Loyalty Points Multiplier

Landmark Rewards members earn a 3x points multiplier on qualifying purchases during both EOSS and the Ramadan campaign, consistent with the loyalty program's standing policy of offering up to 3x points during official campaign periods (typically 3-4 such campaigns per year). The multiplier is applied automatically at checkout based on the transaction date falling within the published campaign window and does not require entry of a separate code. As with all bonus multiplier periods, the 3x rate is communicated in advance via app notification and is never applied retroactively to purchases made before the campaign officially begins.

## Return Window During Sale Events

Items purchased during EOSS or the Ramadan campaign carry a shortened 10-day return window, replacing the brand-specific sale windows that apply outside these events (14 days at Max, 21 days at Splash and Home Centre, 30 days at Lifestyle and Centrepoint). This tighter window reflects the higher return volume and faster restocking cycle Landmark's operations team manages during peak campaign periods, and it applies uniformly across all six brands for the duration of the event, overriding the brand-specific sale-item windows that would otherwise apply. All other return conditions — original tags intact, unworn condition, original packaging — remain unchanged from the standard returns policy. Items purchased before the campaign start date, even if collected or delivered during the campaign window, are still governed by the standard return windows for their original brand and purchase type.

## Doorbuster and Limited-Stock Purchase Limits

Doorbuster items (marked with a "Limited Stock" or "Doorbuster" flag on the product page or in-store signage) are capped at a maximum of 3 units per customer per SKU, and 10 total doorbuster units across all SKUs per customer for the duration of a single campaign. This limit is enforced at checkout by the order system, which flags and blocks any cart attempting to exceed the per-SKU or per-campaign cap, and is tracked at the individual Landmark Rewards account or registered phone number level to prevent circumvention through guest checkout. The purchase limit exists to ensure equitable access to high-demand doorbuster stock rather than allowing bulk resale of deeply discounted items.

## Price-Match Exclusions During Sale Events

The standard price-match guarantee described in the store-and-online-service policy is suspended for the duration of EOSS and the Ramadan campaign. Because markdown depth and category coverage can vary by store location and by day within a fast-moving sale event, Landmark does not honor price-match claims between two Landmark storefronts during these campaigns, nor does it retroactively match a deeper markdown applied later in the same campaign window to an earlier purchase. Customers are advised that if they anticipate a price may drop further as the campaign progresses, they should wait to purchase, since campaign-period price movements are treated as sequential campaign pricing steps rather than price corrections eligible for adjustment.

## Stacking with Other Discounts

Landmark Rewards points redemption can still be applied on top of EOSS and Ramadan campaign markdowns, consistent with the general rule that points combine with running promotions except where explicitly flagged otherwise on the campaign banner. Student and staff discount codes, however, do not apply during these campaigns, since campaign markdowns are treated as sale pricing and the student/staff discount is excluded from already-discounted sale items under its standard terms.
