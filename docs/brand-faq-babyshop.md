# Babyshop Brand FAQ

Babyshop is Landmark Group's kids and baby specialist brand, covering infant and toddler apparel, feeding and hygiene essentials, and baby gear such as strollers and car seats across the UAE, KSA, and India. This supplementary FAQ, maintained by the Babyshop CX team, covers questions specific to safety certification, age-versus-measurement sizing, and hygiene-category returns for baby products.

## Safety Certification Labeling

All Babyshop hard goods (strollers, car seats, cribs, high chairs) display the relevant regional safety certification on the product page and on the physical product label, such as applicable Gulf conformity marks for UAE and KSA listings or BIS certification markers for India listings, alongside the manufacturer's recommended age and weight range for safe use. Customers should always check the certification and recommended weight range against their child's current weight before purchase, since a certification is only valid for use within the stated weight and age bracket, and Babyshop CX cannot authorize a return or exchange based solely on a customer preferring a different certification standard after purchase. Toys and feeding products carry age-grading and choking-hazard warnings on-pack in line with regional toy safety standards, and this labeling should never be removed prior to use.

## Size-by-Age vs. Size-by-Measurement

As explained in the group-wide Size and Fit Guide, Babyshop's infant and toddler clothing sizing is based on both an age-range label (such as 3-6 months) and a corresponding weight bracket in kg, since weight is a materially more reliable fit indicator than age alone for garments in the 0-24 month range, given how much birth weight and growth rate vary between babies. Parents are encouraged to weigh their child and match to the weight-bracket column on the size chart rather than relying on the age label in isolation, particularly for newborn and 0-6 month ranges where growth is fastest. For toddler and older kids' categories carried within Babyshop and Centrepoint's kids corners, sizing shifts to a height-based age-range label (e.g., 4-5Y cross-referenced with height in cm), consistent with the approach used for Centrepoint's kids categories.

## Car Seat and Stroller Warranty and Registration

Babyshop's hard goods category, including strollers and car seats, carries a 12-month warranty on frame and mechanical components such as wheels, folding mechanisms, and harness buckles, but explicitly excludes fabric and foam padding wear after 6 months of documented use, given the accelerated wear cycle typical of baby products, as set out in the group-wide Warranty Policy. Customers purchasing a car seat or stroller are encouraged to register the product with the manufacturer directly (registration details are included in the product packaging) in addition to keeping the Babyshop purchase invoice, since manufacturer registration can be required to process a recall notice or an out-of-warranty goodwill repair, separate from the standard Babyshop warranty claim handled via CX. Warranty claims for car seats and strollers should always include the manufacturer's model and batch number found on the product's safety label, as this is needed to confirm which safety standard revision the specific unit was certified under.

## Hygiene-Related Non-Returnable Categories

Consistent with the group-wide Returns and Exchange Policy's hygiene category rules, opened baby feeding products (bottles, teats, breast pump accessories, sterilizers once used) and opened baby hygiene items (diapers, wipes packs once the seal is broken, bathing and skincare products) are final sale and cannot be returned once the packaging seal has been broken or the product has been used, regardless of reason. This applies even where the product later proves to not suit the baby, since these categories cannot be resold once opened for hygiene reasons. Babyshop CX recommends purchasing a single unit of a new feeding or hygiene product to trial before committing to a multi-pack, precisely because opened multi-packs cannot be partially returned. Unopened, sealed items in original packaging remain eligible for return within the standard 30-day window.
