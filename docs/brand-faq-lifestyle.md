# Lifestyle Brand FAQ

Lifestyle is Landmark Group's flagship department-store brand, carrying fashion, beauty, home accents, and footwear across large-format stores in the UAE, KSA, and India. This supplementary FAQ, maintained by the Lifestyle CX team, covers questions specific to the flagship in-store experience that go beyond the general Returns, Warranty, and Loyalty policies.

## Personal Styling Appointments

Lifestyle flagship stores offer complimentary personal styling appointments, bookable via the "Style Studio" tab on the Lifestyle app or by asking any floor associate in-store. A typical session runs 45-60 minutes and covers wardrobe consultations for occasions such as festive wear, workwear capsules, or seasonal refreshes. Walk-ins are accommodated where a stylist is free, but booking at least 24 hours ahead is recommended at high-footfall stores, particularly during festive and back-to-school periods. Styling sessions are available to all customers regardless of loyalty tier, but Landmark Rewards Platinum members receive a dedicated annual personal shopping session as part of their tier benefits, which is a more extensive, invitation-scheduled version of the standard styling appointment held at a participating Lifestyle or Centrepoint flagship store.

## Premium and Designer Concessions

Lifestyle stores host a curated set of premium and designer concession counters (third-party brands operating shop-in-shop spaces within the Lifestyle floor) spanning contemporary fashion, formalwear, and accessories. Concession stock is subject to the concession brand's own size run and, in some cases, its own return handling if explicitly noted at the counter, though the great majority of concessions follow Lifestyle's standard 30-day return window described in the Returns and Exchange Policy. Customers should check the counter signage or ask the concession staff directly if a specific brand has a different policy, since this is the one area where Lifestyle's otherwise uniform return terms can vary by counter.

## Beauty Hall: Testers and Sampling

The Lifestyle Beauty Hall operates a tester-led sampling model: nearly all fragrance, color cosmetics, and skincare lines on the floor have testers available for in-store trial before purchase, and beauty advisors can apply sample sizes of foundation, skincare, or fragrance on request. Because of hygiene regulations, opened or used cosmetics, fragrances, and skincare products are final sale and cannot be returned once the seal is broken, consistent with the non-returnable hygiene category defined in the general Returns and Exchange Policy. Customers are encouraged to use testers and request samples liberally before committing to a full-size purchase, precisely because post-purchase returns are not possible once a product has been opened.

## Loyalty Tier Perks at Lifestyle Flagship Stores

Beyond the standard Landmark Rewards benefits, Lifestyle flagship stores extend two brand-specific perks noted in the loyalty program terms: complimentary alterations at Lifestyle formalwear counters for Platinum members, and member-only sale preview access 24 hours ahead of public launch for all Silver-tier members and above. Gold and Platinum members also receive early access to new arrivals, which at Lifestyle typically includes first access to new designer concession drops before they are visible to walk-in customers. Points earned or redeemed at Lifestyle pool into the same unified Landmark Rewards balance shared across all six brands, so a customer's Max or Home Centre spend contributes toward the same tier threshold that unlocks these Lifestyle-specific perks.

## Returns at Flagship Stores

Lifestyle honors the full 30-day return window uniformly, including on sale and clearance items, which distinguishes it from Max, Splash, and Home Centre's shorter sale-item windows. This reflects Lifestyle's flagship, premium-anchor positioning within the group, where CX prioritizes a consistent, low-friction return experience over the tighter sale-return cadence used by the value-fashion brands.
